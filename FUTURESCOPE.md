# 🚀 StudyFlow AI - Future Scope & Product Roadmap

## Vision

StudyFlow AI began as an intelligent study planner designed to help students organize tasks, manage revision schedules, and receive AI-powered academic assistance.

The long-term vision is to transform StudyFlow AI into a complete Academic Operating System that supports students throughout their entire learning journey—from planning and studying to revision, assessment, and skill development.

This document outlines the planned future versions and the direction of the platform.

---
